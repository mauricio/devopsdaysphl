How did it work?

Teamwork!

Developers need guidance

You might be able to get someone up to speed alone but that will take time. Grooming new team members requires work.

Wear seatbelts!

Mistakes will be made but you don't want them to affect actual work. You need sane testing environments and automated test suites.

Get on call

Also known as causing pain. Unfortunately, pain is still one of the best sources of education and action.

Having many perspectives

There are many competing interests working at the same project and company, work needs to be done to accomodate these interests.

Cross team, cross functional, cross whatever

No one is alone and moving people around produces better understanding of all the different needs inside a business.

Tearing down the wall

After the fist application was pushed, there was no more separation of the me developer and me ops, when making a decision I had to consider the effects it would have when it was running.

And read Google's SRE book

Even if you're not doing OPS right now, it will change how you write software.
